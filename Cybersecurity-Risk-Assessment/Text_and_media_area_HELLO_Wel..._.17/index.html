HELLO!

Welcome to 
Cybersecurity Risk Assessment & Modelling: Core Priniciples
Thank you for joining us - lets get started...

Whatever your current level of knowledge, we hope that by the end of this course you will have:

  • learnt more about cybersecurity risk assessment
  • understood some of the theory that underpins cybersecurity risk assessment and system modelling
  • developed or expanded your knowledge of cybersecurity concepts 

 

Each stage of the course consists of a Topic. At the start of each Topic is an overview of what will be covered. At the end of each Topic is a quiz to help you check your learning from that Topic. There is no pass or fail for this course, so don't worry, quizzes are not a formal assessment - they are there to help you learn.

When you have finished a Topic click the 'Mark As Done' button and you will automatically progress to the next Topic. 

We Suggest...

If you are:

  • unfamiliar with the fields of cybersecurity and risk assessment, start at the beginning of this course
  • familiar with the basics of cybersecurity and risk assessment, but want to know more about theory jump to Topic 3 of this course
  • expert in cybersecurity and want to know how the Spyderisk tool works, go to Course 2: 'Getting Hands-On with Spyderisk' [ADD LINK ONCE LIVE HERE]
